How It Works

Date-specific weather, beyond 28 days

A simple, practical way to reduce uncertainty when planning far in advance.

Traditional weather forecasts lose reliability beyond about 28 days. At that point, planning usually falls back on long-term averages or guesswork. Weather Ready provides a more useful alternative by generating weather estimations for the exact date you select.

Each estimation blends over 120 years of Perth climate records with recent temperature behaviour to determine what conditions are most likely on that specific day. Instead of offering a broad seasonal outlook, the model produces a single best-estimate maximum temperature that reflects realistic day-to-day variability.

Rainfall is estimated separately using historical patterns for that calendar date, returning both the probability of rain and the expected rainfall amount if rain occurs.

The result is clear, easy-to-understand information â€” maximum temperature, rainfall probability, and rainfall amount â€” designed to be more informative than the long-term average and more reliable than a guess, even months or years ahead.

About

What is Weather Ready?

Weather Ready is a Perth-based, long-range, date-specific weather estimation service designed for planning beyond the ~28-day limit of conventional forecasts. It currently covers the Perth metropolitan area only.

Each purchased estimation is static and reflects the best available data at the time of purchase.

What You Receive

Every Weather Ready estimation includes:

  • Expected maximum temperature
  • Chance of rainfall (%)
  • Estimated rainfall amount (mm)

Clear, practical information for real-world planning.
