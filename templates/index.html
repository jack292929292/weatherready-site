Welcome to Weather Ready – Long-Range Forecast Insights

Weather Ready provides long-range, date-specific weather forecasts for the Perth metro area, including expected maximum temperature and chance of rain.

Key Features of Weather Ready

  1. Date-Specific Forecasting: Forecasts for any specific future date — whether days, months, or even years ahead.
  2. Quantified Accuracy: More than 50% of forecasts fall within ±3°C of the actual maximum temperature for the selected date, regardless of how far away that date is.
  3. Transparency and Accuracy Metrics: See how accurate your forecast is likely to be before making your purchase (see forecast accuracy table below).
  4. Better Accuracy When It Counts: More reliable forecasts on hot and cold days than just relying on the average.
  5. Rainfall Estimates: % chance of rain and likely total if it does — based on historical data from matching condition sets.
  6. Statistically Grounded Method: Uses precision-weighted data blending and random sampling to create more reliable long-range forecasts.

Important Delivery Information

After completing your purchase, your forecast will be automatically emailed to the address you provide. If you do not see the forecast email within a few minutes, please check your junk or spam folder. You may also wish to add weatherreadyinfo@gmail.com to your contacts to help with delivery.

Forecast accuracy is assessed by comparing the predicted maximum temperature to the actual maximum temperature recorded at the Perth Metro weather station (Station ID: 009225, Lat: -31.92, Lon: 115.87, Elevation: 24.9 m), as published by the Bureau of Meteorology (BOM). The ±3°C accuracy statement refers specifically to alignment with this official BOM observation point.

All forecasts are based on observations from the Perth Metro weather station (Station ID: 009225, Lat: -31.92, Lon: 115.87, Elevation: 24.9 m), located in Kings Park. This station is considered representative of the central Perth metropolitan area, including suburbs such as Perth CBD, West Perth, East Perth, Northbridge, Subiaco, Leederville, Mount Lawley, South Perth, Victoria Park, and Burswood. Forecasts may not fully reflect conditions in outer suburbs, coastal zones, or hill districts.
