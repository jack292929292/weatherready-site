Welcome to Weather Ready

Your source for accurate long-range weather forecasting.

Key Features of Weather Ready

  1. Date-Specific Forecasting: Forecasts for any specific future date — whether days, months, or even years ahead.
  2. Quantified Accuracy: More than 50% of forecasts fall within ±3°C of the actual maximum temperature for the selected date, regardless of how far away that date is.
  3. Transparency and Accuracy Metrics: See how accurate your forecast is likely to be before making your purchase (see forecast accuracy table below).
  4. Better Accuracy When It Counts: More reliable forecasts on hot and cold days than just relying on the average.
  5. Rainfall Estimates: % chance of rain and likely total if it does — based on historical data from matching condition sets.
  6. Statistically Grounded Method: Uses precision-weighted data blending and random sampling to create more reliable long-range forecasts.
