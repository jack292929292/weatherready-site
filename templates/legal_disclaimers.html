1. Service Description

Weather Ready provides long-range, date-specific weather forecasts for the Perth metropolitan area. Each forecast includes the predicted maximum temperature, probability of rainfall, and estimated rainfall amount. Forecasts are statistical estimates based on historical weather data and recent trends.

3. Accuracy and Limitations

Forecasts involve inherent uncertainty and may differ from actual weather outcomes. Accuracy is based on statistical alignment with official Bureau of Meteorology records but can vary by location and forecast lead time. Users are responsible for exercising judgment when interpreting forecast results.
