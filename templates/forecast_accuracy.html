About Weather Ready

Weather Ready provides long-range, date-specific weather forecasts for any day more than 14 days into the future. Forecasts are currently available only for the Perth metropolitan area. Each forecast includes the expected maximum temperature, the chance of rainfall (as a percentage), and the estimated rainfall amount in millimetres. Over 50% of forecasts fall within ±3°C of the actual maximum temperature, regardless of lead time. Rainfall predictions are based on historical probabilities.

To receive a forecast, go to weatherready.com.au, select a date more than 14 days away, enter your email, and proceed to payment. Each forecast costs $0.99 AUD. Payments are securely processed using Stripe, supporting credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Once paid, your forecast is shown on-screen and delivered to your inbox within minutes. If it doesn’t appear, please check your spam or junk folder.

Forecasts are static and are not updated once issued. Refunds may be considered in cases of non-delivery or technical issues (see Disclaimer). For support, contact the Weather Ready Live Assistant or email weatherreadyinfo@gmail.com.

You can request forecasts for any date more than 14 days ahead. Forecasts are currently only calibrated for the Perth region.
